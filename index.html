Welcome to DepTrack

DepTrack is a handy dependency management solution to take control over your npm modules.

Just provide your npm package.json file and DepTrack will give you detailed information about your dependencies' latest versions. Take a look at your dependencies' contribution activity like recently opened issues, pull requests and git commits.
