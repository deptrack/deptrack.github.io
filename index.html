~ keep an eye on your dependencies

DepTrack helps you to keep your project dependencies up to date

Just provide your npm package.json file and DepTrack will give you detailed information about your dependencies' latest versions.

Take a look at your dependencies' contribution activity like recently opened issues, pull requests and git commits.
