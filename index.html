Welcome to DepTrack

DepTrack is a handy dependency management tool to take control over your npm modules. Just provide your package.json file for DepTrack, and it will collect everything you need to know about your dependencies.

It helps you to be well-informed about your depencencies' contribution activities, so you are empowered with sufficient information to make responsible decisions about depencency upgrades.
